Our Stores

Garden to Home was founded over 20 years ago, bringing the beauty of flowers into people's lives.

Started with a single store and grew into three locations, each with its own special touch.

Downtown

Opened in 2003, Specialized in custom floral arrangements and event decorations, bringing sophistication and emotion in every petal.

Hours of Operation:

Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Saturday: Closed

Sunday: Closed

Aromas and Colors

Opened in 2010, Focused on fresh flowers and exotic plants, also offering gardening services and home care.

Hours of Operation:

Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 8:00 PM

Saturday: Closed

Sunday: Closed

Flower Garden

Opened in 2018, A tradition in flowers for all occasions, with an exclusive line of dried and scented flowers for decoration and unique gifts.

Hours of Operation:
